Anxiety disorder

Anxiety is normal and common in human life. Occasionally everyone feels anxious in some situations. Fear (one of the six basic emotions) is an immediate emotion to the danger or situation whereas anxiety is a reaction to our emotions, regarding things, we think may happen. (Fear of the future). It becomes a disorder, when it prevents you from leading a good personal, professional and social life.

Mood disorder

Mood is described as an internal feeling that affects almost every element of a person's behavior. It is a temporary state of mind. Affective disorders, often known as mood disorders. These are prevalent mental diseases that result in higher morbidity and death rates. Mood disorders are changes or abnormalities in the mood that affects the person’s ability to lead their life. Mood disorder involves a persistent feeling of either extreme sadness or extreme happiness.
